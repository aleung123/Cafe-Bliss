About Us

Café Bliss was founded by Angela Leung and Hui Lin. On May 1, 2016, we launched our first store in midtown Manhattan, NYC. We are a company that hopes to appeal to a variety of tastes and cravings. Our beverages will help you feel refreshed throughout the day. Our pastries are handmade with effort and care. They are freshly baked in batches everyday to ensure freshness. We serve goods made with the finest ingredients because we want what is best for our customers.
